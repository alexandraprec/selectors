My favorite songs:

Hysteria - Def Leppard

My father loves 80s music, he always plays it and it has been very present in my life. This song is my #1 favorite, I remember my father showing me Def Leppard's music videos and I always paid close attention because I wanted to see the drummer, Rick Allen. If you know anything about Def Leppard and Rick Allen, you understand why, I still believe he is the coolest man ever.

Everybody Wants to Rule the World - Tears for Fears

This song always puts me in a good mood. I could be very stressed but when I hear the intro, I instantly smile and start swaying side to side. This is also a song that I've listened to since childhood. I always feel nostalgic when I listen to it, not just because I've listened to it from a young age but this song has the ability of making me miss the 80s, even though I was born in the year 2000.

Running Up That Hill - Kate Bush

This is not a song that my father ever played, I only started listening to this a few years ago, when it was played on the show Stranger Things. The song is played during a very intense moment on the show and it is definitely the reason why I love this song so much. As you may have noticed, I love 80s music as much as my father does and this song just fits in perfectly with the rest on this list.
